Dr Yussuf Motari -Founder & Patron

CPA Accounting

At this Academy, I have demonstrated my love and commitment to my calling as a lecturer. I dedicate my time, resources, and effort to supporting my mentees. I founded the Academy in 2024 with an inaugural cohort of 20 mentees, driven by a vision to create a space where young people could be guided, inspired, and equipped to reach their full potential. Since then, we have continued to grow—making a yearly call for new mentees who are eager to learn, lead, and make a difference in their communities. Through our carefully designed programs, I have worked to build an environment that goes beyond conventional learning—fostering critical thinking, integrity, innovation, and leadership. It has been deeply rewarding to witness the transformation of our mentees as they gain confidence, develop essential skills, and take bold steps toward their aspirations. Many have gone on to earn awards and recognition through their innovative projects and impactful initiatives. My ongoing commitment is to ensure that Motari Mentorship Academy remains a beacon of guidance, growth, and opportunity for all who pass through its doors.
